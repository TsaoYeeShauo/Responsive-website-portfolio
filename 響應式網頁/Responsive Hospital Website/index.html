Our Services

All of this is delivered in some of the newest health care facilities in the region designed for the comfort and convenience of our patients and their families.

Community Health

Population health centers on improving health outcomes of families by encouraging them to practice healthy lifestyles.

Emergency Room

Our 36-bed Emergency Room is staffed 24-hours a day, seven days a week by board-certified Johns Hopkins emergency medicine physicians. The 24,000 square foot expansion provides state-of-the-art comprehensive, individualized emergency medical care and urgent care to the citizens of Howard County and the surrounding area.

Heart and Vascular

Heart disease is the number one cause of death in the United States. Howard County General Hospital provides a wide range of services for people with heart and vascular disease: from vascular surgery to the educational programs in our Wellness Center to our Cardiac and Vascular Diagnostic Programs to our treatment and rehabilitation services.

Obstetrics

Having a baby is a unique and special time in a woman's life, and at Howard County General Hospital: A Member of Johns Hopkins Medicine, we do everything possible to make that experience safe, comfortable and happy.

Howard County General Hospital

Nothing is more important to parents than the health of their children. When a child is sick or has an accident, they need to get the appropriate level of professional help in a timely way and caring environment. We recognizes the unique physical and emotional requirements of children and infants and has developed programs to meet their short and long-term health needs.

24 Hour Service

The hospital provides emergency, inpatient and observation care for children 24-hours a day, seven days a week.
